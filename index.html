+
A custom website (design, hosting, SEO, AI chat, listing feed), an ongoing organic social + article engine, and managed paid social on Instagram and Facebook. Every service is delivered by a dedicated human marketing manager backed by our AI stack. No templates, no shared inventory.

+
Extra. The $99 for Targeted Social Ads covers our management, creative, and reporting. The $200 is paid directly to Meta (Instagram/Facebook) as the actual media spend and is what activates the lead guarantee.

Organic content begins indexing within weeks. Paid social typically produces inquiries inside of 30 days once the pixel is seasoned. If we haven't hit leads by month two under the full bundle, we continue at no cost until we do.

No. Everything is month-to-month through Wave. Cancel any time with two weeks' notice.
