PYY Marine07 / 12
The core shift

Stop renting your buyers from the marketplaces.

Boats.com, Boat Trader and YachtWorld send leads — but you pay per lead, that lead is shared with every competitor on the page, and you never keep the buyer's data. The goal isn't to drop them; it's to stop depending on them.

Renting · today
Marketplace leads
  • Pay per lead, every month, forever
  • The same buyer is sold to your competitors
  • No email, no pixel, no data you keep
  • You can't retarget or follow up on your own
Owning · the plan
Your own audience
  • Leads come to your site, social & CRM — exclusively yours
  • Every visitor is pixeled and re-targetable
  • Emails & phone numbers you keep and remarket to
  • Cost per lead falls as your audience compounds

Keep the marketplaces as a top-of-funnel source — but make your own channels the engine that owns the buyer.
