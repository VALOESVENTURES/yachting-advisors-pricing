SMS Consent & Opt-In Process

This page documents how Yachting Advisors collects express written consent from its business clients before sending SMS/text messages, and the information each client receives at the time of opt-in.

Section 01

Program overview.

  • Who receives messages. Only existing, paying business clients of Yachting Advisors (yacht brokerages and yacht-industry businesses). This is a business-to-business communications program. No consumer-facing opt-ins, no purchased lists, no marketing blasts to the general public.
  • How consent is collected. Each client completes an intake form during onboarding, after signing a service agreement. The intake form contains a dedicated SMS opt-in section (shown below) in which the client affirmatively checks a box and provides their mobile number. Consent is captured in writing and stored with the client's account record.
  • What messages contain. Lead notifications (new inquiries generated by campaigns we manage), account updates (billing reminders, service milestones), and service communications (scheduling, reporting, operational updates from the client's dedicated marketing manager).
  • Expected message frequency. Varies by account activity. Typically 1–10 messages per month per client. Across the full client base this is approximately 20 messages per day or fewer.
  • Opt-out method. Clients may reply STOP to any message at any time to be unsubscribed, or reply HELP for support information. We also honor opt-out requests made directly to their account manager.
